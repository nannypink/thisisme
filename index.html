About me

Engineer, community builder, lifelong learner

I'm a Software Engineer at NatWest Group, working within Engineering Platforms on the intersection of technology and business outcomes. My day-to-day spans data engineering, microservice development, CI/CD automation, and executive reporting — but what really drives me is the people side: building communities, developing junior talent, and making engineering more accessible.

Before moving into tech full-time, I ran my own business delivering bookkeeping and digital services to SMEs, spent time managing operations across 20 healthcare sites, and even taught floristry at college. That breadth isn't a quirk — it's given me commercial awareness, people skills, and adaptability that purely technical backgrounds can lack.

I'm currently deepening my expertise in cloud-native development, SAFe product ownership, and local agentic AI — always curious, always building. I made Clawdia, and Clawdia made me this site.
